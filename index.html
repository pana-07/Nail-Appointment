About Polished.

We're a boutique nail studio built around one belief: self-care should feel like a ritual, not a routine. Every appointment begins with a conversation and ends with nails you genuinely love.

Hygiene is non-negotiable — we use single-use files, hospital-grade sterilization, and premium products. Our team is fully certified in Gel-X application and trained in contemporary nail artistry.
